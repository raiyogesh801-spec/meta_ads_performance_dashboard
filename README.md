# meta_ads_performance_dashboard
Power BI dashboard analyzing Meta advertising campaign performance across Facebook and Instagram to track engagement, audience behavior, conversions, and campaign effectiveness.

**Project Overview**

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing advertising campaign performance across Facebook and Instagram.

The dashboard tracks engagement, conversions, audience behavior, and campaign spending to help evaluate how effectively ads reach users and convert them into customers.

**Business Objective**

The goal of this project is to provide marketing teams with a clear view of campaign performance by answering key questions:

Which audiences engage the most with ads?

Which ad formats perform best?

Which countries generate the highest engagement?

When are users most active?

These insights help optimize campaign strategy and budget allocation.

**Key Metrics**

The dashboard tracks important digital marketing KPIs including:

Impressions – Total ad views

Clicks – User interactions with ads

Engagements – Combined interactions (clicks, shares, comments)

Purchases – Conversions generated from ads

CTR (Click Through Rate)

Engagement Rate

Conversion Rate

Purchase Rate

Campaign Budget Metrics


**Dashboard Insights**

Key insights from the analysis include:

Campaigns achieved high reach and strong engagement levels.

Young adult audiences show the highest engagement.

Female users contribute a significant share of interactions.

Video and Story ads outperform other ad formats.

Engagement peaks during afternoon and evening hours.

Strong engagement but lower purchase conversion, suggesting opportunities to improve the conversion funnel.

**Tools Used**

Power BI – Dashboard development

Power Query – Data transformation

DAX – KPI calculations and metrics

**Project Outcome**

This dashboard demonstrates how marketing data can be transformed into actionable insights, helping organizations understand audience behavior, optimize campaign strategies, and improve advertising performance.
